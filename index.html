★ HOW TO PLAY ★
🎲
ROLL
Tap your dice on your turn. It always lands on 1, 3, 5, 7, or 9.
🏗️
BUILD
Roll the same number 5 times to fully build a stickman in that slot.
💥
EARN BULLET
Completing a stickman sends a bullet flying into your victory bar.
🏆
WIN
First to hold 3 bullets at the same time wins the match!
STICKMAN ASSEMBLY — 5 STAGES
⚡ ATTACK MODE: Instead of rolling, click one of your earned bullets. Your opponent's active slots start shaking — click any one to destroy their progress and reset to zero. Costs 1 bullet!
💡 TIP: Build all 5 slots at once. Only attack when your opponent is close to winning — spending bullets carelessly means you need to rebuild to 3!
